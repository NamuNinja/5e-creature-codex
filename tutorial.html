üîç Searching & Filtering Creatures

1

Use the Search Bar

Type any part of a creature's name in the search box at the top. Results update instantly as you type.

Example: Type "dragon" to see all dragons, or "anc" to find ancient creatures
2

Filter by Challenge Rating (CR)

Click the CR filter buttons to show only creatures within specific difficulty ranges:

  • CR 0-4: Low-level encounters
  • CR 5-10: Mid-tier challenges
  • CR 11-16: High-level threats
  • CR 17+: Epic encounters
3

Filter by Creature Type

Select from dropdown to filter by type: Beast, Dragon, Undead, Humanoid, Aberration, etc.

4

Toggle Custom Creatures

Use the "Show Custom Only" toggle to view only your custom-created creatures, or turn it off to see SRD creatures.

5

Combine Filters

Use multiple filters together! Search for "dragon" + CR 17+ to find only ancient dragons.

üìñ Viewing Creature Details

1

Open Creature Modal

Click on any creature card to open the detailed stat block in a modal window.

2

Read the Stat Block

The modal displays all creature information:

  • Size, Type, Alignment
  • Armor Class (AC) and Hit Points (HP)
  • Speed (walking, flying, swimming, etc.)
  • Ability Scores (STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS, CHA)
  • Saving Throws and Skills
  • Damage Resistances, Immunities, Vulnerabilities
  • Senses and Languages
  • Special Abilities
  • Actions and Legendary Actions
3

Add to Battle Preparation

Use the "Battle Preparation Tabs" feature to add this creature to combat tracking and HP management.

4

Close Modal

Click the X button at the top right of the modal to close it.

‚ù§Ô∏è Using Battle Preparation Tabs

1

Open Desired Creature Card

Click the creature card you wish to add to the battle tabs section to access HP tracking and combat management.

2

Add Creatures to Track

Click the "Add To Battle Tab" button at the top of the creature card to add it to your battle tracker.

3

Apply Damage

For each tracked creature:

  • Click the red "Damage" button
  • Enter damage amount
  • HP automatically decreases
  • Progress bar updates to show remaining HP
4

Apply Healing

Click the green "Heal" button and enter healing amount. HP cannot exceed maximum.

5

Add Temporary HP

Click "Temp HP" button to add temporary hit points. Damage is applied to temp HP first.

6

Reset or Remove

Options for managing tracked creatures:

  • Reset HP: Restores creature to full HP
  • Remove: Removes creature from tracker
  • Clear All: Removes all tracked creatures

üí° Pro Tip: Battle Preparation data is automatically saved to your browser, so you can close and reopen the app without losing your combat progress!

‚ú® Creating Custom Creatures

1

Click "+ Add Creature"

Find the "+ Add Creature" button at the bottom right corner of the screen (both mobile and desktop).

2

Fill in Basic Info

Enter creature's basic statistics:

  • Name (required)
  • Type (beast, humanoid, etc.)
  • Size (tiny, small, medium, large, huge, gargantuan)
  • Alignment
  • Challenge Rating (CR)
3

Add Combat Stats

Enter:

  • Armor Class (AC)
  • Hit Points (HP)
  • Speed
  • Ability Scores (STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS, CHA)
4

Add Abilities & Actions

Use the text areas to describe special abilities, actions, and legendary actions.

5

Save Your Creature

Click "Save" to add the creature. It will appear with a purple "CUSTOM" badge.

6

Edit or Delete

Open a custom creature's detail modal to find Edit and Delete buttons.

üí° Note: Custom creatures are saved locally to your browser/device. They won't sync across different devices or browsers.
