General Questions

What is 5E Creature Codex?

5E Creature Codex is a comprehensive reference tool for Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition creatures. It contains 322+ creatures from the SRD 5.1, allowing you to quickly search, filter, and reference creature stats during your game sessions.

Is this free to use?

Yes! 5E Creature Codex is completely free to use on both web and mobile platforms. Web advertising helps cover hosting and infrastructure costs, while 100% of advertising revenue from the mobile app is donated to Extra Life (Children's Miracle Network Hospitals).

What platforms is this available on?

5E Creature Codex is available on:

  • Web: Access at 5e-creature-codex.org from any browser
  • Android: Coming Soon!
  • iOS: Coming soon!

Features & Usage

Can I add my own custom creatures?

Yes! You can create custom creatures with the "+ Add Creature" button (bottom right corner). All custom creatures are saved locally in your browser and marked with a purple "CUSTOM" badge to distinguish them from SRD creatures.

⚠️ Important: Always backup your custom creatures using the Import/Export feature (bottom right corner). If you clear your browser data or cache, you will lose all custom creatures permanently.

How do the Battle Preparation Tabs work?

Battle Preparation Tabs allow you to track multiple creatures' hit points during combat. Open a creature card and click "Add To Battle Tab" to add it to your battle tracker. You can then apply damage, healing, and even track temporary HP. All tracked creatures persist until you manually remove them or clear all.

How do I filter creatures?

Use the filter buttons to narrow down creatures by:

  • Challenge Rating (CR): Filter by difficulty level
  • Type: Filter by creature type (beast, dragon, undead, etc.)
  • Search: Search by name or description
  • Custom toggle: Show only custom creatures or only SRD creatures

Does this work offline?

The mobile app works fully offline once loaded. The website requires an internet connection to load initially, but all creature data is loaded in your browser, so you can browse creatures without additional data usage after the initial page load.

Technical Support

My data isn't saving. What's wrong?

Custom creatures and HP tracker data are saved to your browser's local storage. If data isn't saving:

  • Make sure you're not in private/incognito mode
  • Check that cookies and local storage are enabled
  • Try clearing your browser cache and reloading
  • On mobile, ensure the app has storage permissions

The app isn't loading properly. Help!

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Web: Clear browser cache, refresh page (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
  • Mobile: Force close app, clear app cache, restart device
  • Update: Make sure you have the latest version
  • Browser: Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
